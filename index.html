WHAT IS ITURE?

ITURE( In-Term Undergraduate Research Engagement ) serves as a centralized platform for allocating research projects to motivated undergraduate students. It enables them to pursue projects within their respective departments and facilitates the initiation of interdisciplinary projects aligned with their interests. By fostering collaboration with professors and their teams at IIT Kanpur, it encourages students to engage in hands-on research experiences throughout the semester actively. development of the IITK Research Portal to streamline the process of mapping professors with students for projects. This initiative aims to address the cumbersome and time-consuming project allocation system. necessity of this portal, highlighting its potential to help students secure projects even mid-semester, and particularly for the SURGE program, which previously required students to secure professors four months in advance.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I apply for projects? +
  • Go to the Portal and click on the APPLY NOW tab present besides the description of the specific project you want to apply for. You will be directed to the google form where you will be required to submit your personal details and some specific details as mentioned by the professor and SoP.
What should I write in an SOP? +
  • Statement of Purpose should reflect the motivation with which you are filling out the application for the project you wish to do. It is used by the professor to judge if you are enthusiastic enough to learn and work for the entire duration of the project. Please write a separate SOP directed towards each project in case you are applying for 2 projects.
What is the duration of the IITK Research projects? +
  • The project durations listed are tentative and may extend beyond the initial timeframe. The length of the IITK Research project is determined mutually by you and the professor. During the interview, you can propose your preferred dates, and the professor will provide feedback on their availability.
What would be the penalty if I leave the project in between? +
  • Any student who fails to perform satisfactorily in the project and/or leaves the project without the consent of the Professor-in-charge will be blacklisted in all further initiatives by ANC Council. They will not receive any certification for the program
What would be the criteria for getting the certificate for project? +
  • At the end of their project, the Professor would review their work and give it a grade out of 10, and anyone >= 6 will get a satisfactory certificate for IITK Research project, and those >=8 will get a certificate of excellence.
