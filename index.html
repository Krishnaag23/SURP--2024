IITK PROJECTS PORTAL

WHAT IS IITK Projects Portal?

IITK Projects Portal serves as a centralized platform for allocating research projects to motivated undergraduate students. It enables them to pursue projects within their respective departments and facilitates the initiation of interdisciplinary projects aligned with their interests. By fostering collaboration with professors and their teams at IIT Kanpur, it encourages students to engage in hands-on research experiences throughout the semester actively. The motivation behind the development of the IITK Projects Portal to streamline the process of mapping professors with students for projects. This initiative aims to address the cumbersome and time-consuming project allocation system, highlighting its potential to help students secure projects even mid-semester, and particularly for the SURGE program, which previously required students to secure professors four months in advance.



IITK Projects 2024 SELECTION PROCEDURE

Stage 1:

To apply for a project, you need to submit an individual application form for each project you are interested in. Please ensure that you provide all required information, including the Statement of Purpose (SOP) and other specified requirements as mentioned in the form.

Stage 2:

Professors will review the applications and select suitable candidates. Shortlisted candidates might be required to give interviews or tests.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I apply for projects? +
  • Go to the Projects page and look for the department under which you want your project. Over there, open the details for the project. In the bottom-right corner, click on the APPLY NOW tab present below the description of the specific project you want to apply for. You will be directed to the google form where you will be required to submit your personal details along with your SoP and resume.
What should I write in an SOP? +
  • Statement of Purpose should reflect the motivation with which you are filling out the application for the project you wish to do. It is used by the professor to judge if you are enthusiastic enough to learn and work for the entire duration of the project. Please write a separate SOP directed towards each project in case you are applying for multiple projects.
What is the duration of the projects? +
  • The project durations listed are tentative and may extend beyond the initial timeframe. The length of the project is determined mutually by you and the professor. During the interview, you can propose your preferred dates, and the professor will provide feedback on their availability.
What would be the penalty if I leave the project in between? +
  • Any student who fails to perform satisfactorily in the project and/or leaves the project without the consent of the Professor-in-charge will be blacklisted in all further initiatives by ANC Council. They will not receive any certification for the program
What would be the criteria for getting the certificate for project? +
  • At the end of their project, the Professor would review their work and give it a grade out of 10, and anyone >= 6 will get a satisfactory certificate for the project, and those >=8 will get a certificate of excellence.
